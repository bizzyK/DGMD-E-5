Ways You Can Help

  • Donate: Support the production and distribution of our documentary.

  • Partner: Collaborate with us as a school, nonprofit, or cultural organization.
  • Share: Spread the word by sharing our story on social media and in your community.

About Our Organization

Our River Initiative is a project of FEANE Charities, a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible and directly support the completion of our documentary and the development of educational programming related to the Connecticut River.
