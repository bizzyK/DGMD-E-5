Project Summary

The Connecticut: Our River is a documentary film that depicts the natural wonder of the river from its source in Canada, along its journey through four New England states, to its meeting with the salt water of Long Island Sound. The personal and professional reflections of interviewees enlighten and engage viewers in the battle for a clean Connecticut River. It emphasizes the ongoing struggle to maintain and improve river quality and access. The film balances the pristine beauty of the river against the pollution, invasive species, sewage runoff, garbage dumps, and dead beat dams that spoil the river and threaten its future.
