Project Summary

Our River: Picking Up the Story is a multimedia documentary project created to relaunch and support the completion of The Connecticut: Our River, a film originally begun by my father. His documentary follows the Connecticut River from its source in Canada to Long Island Sound, focusing on the balance between natural beauty and environmental degradation. My project continues that mission by highlighting the moment I chose to step in and carry the story forward after his work was paused by health and family challenges. Through new video, photography, and personal storytelling, I aim to reengage audiences and lay the foundation for a nonprofit dedicated to securing funding and visibility for the filmâ€™s completion.
